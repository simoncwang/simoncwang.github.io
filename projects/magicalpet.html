About the project

This friendly looking turtle was created over the course of a semester for CMSC730 - Interactive Technology in Human-Computer Interaction. I led a group of three to conceptualize and develop a robot that fulfilled the following simple requirements; a novel robot, tangible interface, or haptic device involving both hardware and software components, and above all, is interactive!
