Creating Multi-Agent Systems with SmolAgents

Cracking the HuggingFace Agents Course Final Certification!

Introduction

I recently successfully completed the full certification from the Hugging Face AI Agents Course. As the final project of this course, I created a multi-agent system using SmolAgents to solve complex agentic reasoning problems from the GAIA dataset. Ultimately, through extensive system design, testing, and prompt engineering, I was able to achieve a 55% success rate on the required subset of the GAIA dataset (passing was 30%)!

The course

This free course from Hugging Face is a great introduction to the world of AI agents, and covers a wide range of topics including:

  • the fundamentals of agentic AI systems (LLMs, messages & tokens, tools, reasoning, Re-Act)
  • agent frameworks (smolagents, LangGraph, LLamaIndex)
  • use cases (e.g. agentic RAG)
  • a practical final project to create and design an agentic system to solve GAIA reasoning problems

This post focuses on my experience with approaching the final project, including the design and engineering of my system, as well as the lessons I learned along the way. If you are interested in learning more about the course, I highly recommend checking it out and perhaps taking a stab at it yourself here!
